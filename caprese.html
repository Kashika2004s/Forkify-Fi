Caprese Salad with Pesto Sauce Recipe

About Caprese Salad with Pesto sauce Recipe: Nothing like a fresh tomato salad in summers!
A great antipasto bite to start your meal with. This combination of juicy tomatoes and
mozzarella cheese salad topped with freshly made pesto sauce is a distinct yet simple one.
It offers a twist to the classic caprese salad.

Ingredients:


    Ingredients for Salad:

    • 2 Tomatoes
    • Handful of Tulsi
    • 1 packet Buffalo Mozzarella
    • 1/4 cup Balsamic Vinegar
    • Salt and Pepper to taste
    • 1 cup Cherry Tomatoes, halved
    • 1/2 Red Onion, thinly sliced
    • 1 Avocado, sliced

    Ingredients for Pesto Sauce:

    • Handful of Basil
    • 1 cup Pine nuts
    • 1 cup Parmesan Cheese, grated
    • 1/2 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil
    • Juice of half a Lemon
    • 2 Garlic cloves
    • 1/4 cup Pecorino Romano Cheese, grated
    • 1/2 teaspoon Salt

How to Make Caprese Salad with Pesto Sauce



To prepare Caprese Salad:

  1. Slice the tomatoes and Buffalo Mozzarella.

  2. Arrange tomato and mozzarella slices on a plate, alternating with Cherry Tomatoes and Avocado.

  3. Sprinkle Tulsi and Red Onion on top.

  4. Add green chillies & ginger & cook till saag gets thick.

  5. Drizzle 1/4 cup Balsamic Vinegar over the salad. Add Salt and Pepper to taste.

  6. Optionally, garnish with additional Tulsi leaves.


To prepare Pesto Salad:

    • In a food processor, combine Basil, Pine nuts, and grated Parmesan Cheese.
    • Pulse until the ingredients are finely chopped.
    • With the processor running, gradually add Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
    • Continue blending until the mixture forms a smooth paste.
    • Squeeze the Juice of half a Lemon into the pesto.
    • Add salt to taste (typically around 1/2 teaspoon).
