Manchurian typically refers to a popular Indo-Chinese dish known
as "Gobi Manchurian", depending on whether it's made with cauliflower (Gobi)
or chicken. It's a dish that originated in the Indian Chinese community and is widely
enjoyed in India and other parts of the world.


Manchurian is a delicious fusion of Chinese cooking techniques and Indian
flavors, making it a favorite in many households and restaurants, especially
among those who enjoy the unique taste of Indo-Chinese cuisine.

Ingredients:


    Ingredients for batter:

  • 1 medium-sized cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped garlic
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped ginger
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 medium bell pepper (capsicum)
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch mixed with 1/4 cup water
  • Chopped spring onions for garnish

How to Make Manchurian



To prepare Manchurian:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour, cornstarch, ginger-garlic paste,
    soy sauce, red chili powder, and salt.

  2. Add water gradually to form a smooth batter, neither too thick nor too thin.

  3. Dip the cauliflower florets in the batter, ensuring they are evenly coated.

  4. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat. Fry the cauliflower florets
    in batches until they are golden brown and crispy.

  5. Remove from oil and drain excess oil on paper towels. Set aside.In,
    another pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil over medium-high heat.

    fragrant.Add chopped onions and bell peppers. Cook until they are soft and
    translucent.

    pepper. Stir well to combine.

  8. Pour in the cornstarch slurry and cook until the sauce thickens, stirring
    continuously.

  9. Add the fried cauliflower florets to the sauce. Toss well to coat the florets
    evenly with the sauce.

  10. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.
